Cheatsheet

About Vimstructor

Vimstructor is an offline-friendly, browser-hosted game that teaches you the Vim text editor. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your progress is saved in your browser via localStorage.

How it works

  • Each chapter introduces one topic and gives you hands-on exercises.
  • You interact with a simulated Vim editor that recognizes real Vim commands.
  • Complete the goal shown on the left panel to unlock the next lesson.

Tips

  • Click the editor (or press Tab) to focus it before typing.
  • Press Esc to return to Normal mode any time.
  • Stuck? Click Hint on the lesson panel.
